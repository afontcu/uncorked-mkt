Overview

Uncorked is a personal wine cataloging app. Your data stays on your device. We don't have accounts, servers, or any way to access your information.

What Data We Collect

Wine Photos

When you photograph a wine label, the image is sent to Google's AI service (Gemini) to extract wine information (name, winery, region, etc.). Google processes this data according to their privacy policy.

Your Wine Collection

All wines, tasting notes, ratings, and other data you enter are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't create user accounts
  • We don't sync data to any server
  • We don't track your behavior or location
  • We don't show advertisements
  • We don't sell or share your data
  • We don't use behavioral analytics or tracking SDKs

Data Storage & Deletion

Your wine collection is stored on your iPhone using Apple's on-device database. To delete your data, simply delete the app ‚Äî all data is removed with it.
